The Blood Donation Process

Donating blood is a simple and safe process that takes about an hour and can save lives.

1. Registration

You’ll fill out a donor form with basic information and a brief health history.

2. Health Check

Medical staff will check your blood pressure, hemoglobin, and general wellness.

3. Donation

A sterile needle is used to collect about 350-450ml of blood. The process usually takes 10-15 minutes.

4. Rest & Refreshments

After donation, you’ll be given snacks and juice to help you recover. Most people feel fine in minutes.

5. Recovery

It’s recommended to drink water, eat well, and avoid heavy activity for a few hours.
